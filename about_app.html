About App


This is a Streamlit app designed to help you find the best courses on Coursera quickly and easily. With its user-friendly and interactive interface, it allows users to search and filter top results based on their preferences. Whether you're looking for short courses or in-depth programs, the app provides options to filter by course duration and difficulty levels.

Here's what makes it stand out
  • Simple and intuitive design: You don't need to be tech-savvy to use it. Everything is straightforward.
  • Selection filters: You can easily customize your search based on how long you want the course to be or how challenging you want it.
  • Fast and efficient: It scrapes the top results from Coursera, so you get up-to-date and relevant course recommendations.

It's the perfect tool to find your next online course with minimal hassle!
